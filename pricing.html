Frequently Asked Questions

What payment methods are accepted?
Vietnamese users: bank transfer via QR VietQR (SePay). International users: Visa, Mastercard, PayPal via Paddle. All transactions are processed securely.
How long does it take to receive my report?
Your PDF report is delivered to your email within 10 minutes of confirmed payment. If you don't receive it within 30 minutes, please contact us.
Is this an official valuation certificate?
No. ValuitBiz provides automated mathematical simulations for internal reference and deal preparation purposes only. Results do not substitute official valuation certificates regulated by the Ministry of Finance of Vietnam (Bộ Tài chính).
Can I get a refund?
Due to the digital nature of the product (instant PDF delivery), refunds are generally not available once the report has been generated. If there is a technical error preventing delivery, please contact support within 24 hours.
What valuation methods are used?
Berkus & Scorecard (Startups), Market Multiples P/E·P/S·P/B + Gordon Growth Model (SMEs), DCF/FCFF + WACC + EV/EBITDA (Enterprise), and Comparable Sales + Income (Cap Rate) + Cost Approach (Real Estate).
Is my data secure?
Yes. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. We comply with Vietnam's Decree 13/2023/NĐ-CP on personal data protection. We do not sell or share your financial data with third parties.
⚠️ ValuitBiz valuation outputs are automated financial simulations intended for internal reference and pre-deal preparation only. Results do not constitute official appraisals or valuation certificates under regulations of the Ministry of Finance of Vietnam (Bộ Tài chính — Nghị định 30/2021/NĐ-CP). Engage a licensed appraiser for legally binding valuations.
